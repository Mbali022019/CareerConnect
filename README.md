# CareerConnect

## Intelligent Job Matching and Recruitment Platform

CareerConnect is a full-stack web-based employment platform designed to connect job seekers with relevant employment opportunities through intelligent and data-driven features. The system helps users upload CVs, receive job recommendations, analyse skill gaps, and improve employability, while employers can efficiently manage recruitment and applications.

---

# Problem Statement

Many job seekers struggle to:

* find suitable employment opportunities
* identify required industry skills
* create competitive CVs
* avoid fraudulent job advertisements

At the same time, employers face challenges in:

* filtering large numbers of applications
* identifying qualified candidates efficiently
* managing recruitment processes digitally

CareerConnect aims to solve these problems through an intelligent recruitment platform that improves job matching, employability, and recruitment efficiency.
